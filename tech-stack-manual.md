# Tech Stack Manual

## 1. System Overview
It tracks students and cohorts for NSS, it keeps track of the number of students per cohort and what lesson they are on currently. There is also a users detail page when you click on their name, it shows what learning objectives they have remaining, their github information, and buttons to delete or transfer the student to another cohort. There is a teams page to assign teams for certain assignments or projects, also the ability to delete current teams. It appears to be used for teachers at NSS to keep track of students and their cohorts.
